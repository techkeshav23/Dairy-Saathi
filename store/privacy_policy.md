Welcome to **MY ORDER PRO**, a business-to-business (B2B) billing and ordering application developed and operated by **Mail Blimp Software Development Pvt Ltd** ("we," "us," or "our").

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless billing and ordering experience, we collect the following types of information:

### A. Information You Provide to Us
*   **Authentication Data:** We use phone number authentication via One-Time Password (OTP) to verify your identity and secure your account.
*   **Business Profile Data:** Information you enter to set up your business profile, such as your business name, address, tax identification numbers, and contact details.
*   **Customer & Invoice Data:** As a B2B app, you may input data regarding your own clients/customers (e.g., names, phone numbers, addresses) and financial data (e.g., invoices, order histories, product catalogs, and pricing). *Note: You act as the Data Controller for your customers' data, and we act as the Data Processor.*

### B. Information Collected Automatically
*   **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers) to ensure app compatibility and troubleshoot bugs.
*   **Usage Data:** Information on how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, crash logs) to improve performance and user experience.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:
*   **To Provide Core Functionality:** To authenticate your account via OTP, enable you to create and manage invoices, process orders, and maintain your business catalog.
*   **To Secure Your Account:** To prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and ensure the overall security of the App.
*   **To Improve the App:** To analyze crash logs and usage trends so we can fix bugs and develop new features.
*   **To Communicate with You:** To send you important service updates, security alerts, and support messages.

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data or your customers' data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.**

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
Your data is securely stored using **Supabase**, a robust, hosted PostgreSQL database infrastructure. Supabase employs industry-standard security practices to ensure your data is highly available and protected against unauthorized access.

### How Your Data is Protected
*   **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the MY ORDER PRO app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL protocols. Data is also encrypted at rest within our database.
*   **Access Controls:** Access to production databases is strictly limited to authorized personnel at Mail Blimp Software Development Pvt Ltd on a need-to-know basis for maintenance and support.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

To operate the App effectively, we integrate with trusted third-party service providers. These providers only process your data as necessary to perform their specific functions and are bound by strict data protection agreements:

*   **Supabase:** Used for backend database hosting (PostgreSQL) and authentication services (OTP delivery and session management).
*   **Google Play Services:** Used for app distribution, crash reporting, and standard Android analytics.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account information and business data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. 

### Your Right to Delete Data
In compliance with Google Play’s Data Deletion requirements, you have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

**How to request data deletion:**
1.  **In-App:** Navigate to `Settings` > `Account` > `Delete Account` within the MY ORDER PRO app.
2.  **Via Email:** Send an email to [Insert Email Address] with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" from your registered email address, including your registered phone number.

**What happens when you request deletion:**
Upon receiving a deletion request, we will permanently delete your account, phone number, business profile, and all associated customer/invoice data from our active Supabase databases within 30 days. *(Note: We may retain certain data strictly where required by law for tax, legal, or accounting purposes).*

*   **Right to Erasure:** As detailed in Section 5, you can request the deletion of your data.
