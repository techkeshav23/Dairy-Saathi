Whether you need to generate professional GST invoices, manage B2B orders from retailers, track inventory, or maintain your digital ledger (Udhar Khata), MY ORDER PRO streamlines your entire business workflow from a single app.

*   Create and share GST or non-GST bills in seconds via WhatsApp or Email.
*   Customize invoices with your business logo, signature, and terms.
*   Choose from multiple professional invoice formats.
*   Easily generate E-way bills and E-invoices directly from the app.

*   Empower your retailers to place orders directly through a digital catalog.
*   Track order status (Pending, Dispatched, Delivered) in real-time.

*   Manage wholesale pricing, bulk discounts, and scheme offers effortlessly.

*   Track stock levels across multiple godowns or warehouses.
*   Get low-stock alerts to ensure you never run out of fast-moving items.
*   Manage item variants (size, color) and batch numbers with expiry dates.
*   Perform quick stock adjustments and barcode scanning.
